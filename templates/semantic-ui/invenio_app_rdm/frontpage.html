The UNESCO Open Science Platform is your open gateway to the knowledge produced by the UNESCO Natural Sciences Family. It provides open access to original publications, policy briefs, reports, data, and related information published or commissioned by UNESCO and its partners. The platform also provides thematic insights from these studies through natural language summaries and dashboards.
