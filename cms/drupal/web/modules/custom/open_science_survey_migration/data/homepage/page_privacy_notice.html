Your privacy is important to us. We take every precaution to protect the information you provide in accordance with the UNESCO Principles on Personal Data Protection and Privacy (https://www.unesco.org/en/privacy-policy).

In connection to system analytics and monitoring on the UNESCO Open Science Platform, UNESCO collects and processes information including personal data such as your behavioural data (e.g., clickstream data and app usage patterns), location data (e.g., IP addresses), and log data (e.g., system logs and error logs).

The information will be processed only for the purpose of ensuring the security, reliability, and performance of the platform and for producing aggregated usage statistics to help UNESCO make informed decisions for its continuous improvement. Access to the information will be strictly limited to UNESCO.

The information you provide will be processed exclusively for the purposes described in this notice. Any other use will always be subject to further consent.

We will not process your personal data for direct marketing or other commercial purposes.

These data will be stored for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this privacy notice, in accordance with our applicable records retention schedules.

If you have any questions about how your personal data are processed in connection with your use of the UNESCO Open Science Platform, please send an email to: open-science-platform@unesco.org.

For any other questions regarding the processing of your personal data, contact privacy@unesco.org.
