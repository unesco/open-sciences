Beyond the programmes and staff at UNESCO Headquarters in Paris and in the Field Offices worldwide, the UNESCO-related institutes, centres, ambassadors, and networks are an integral part of the UNESCO Natural Sciences Family. They serve as unique relays for UNESCO action across the globe.

The UNESCO Natural Sciences Family includes:

  • The programmes and staff of the Natural Sciences Sector: operating at UNESCO Headquarters in Paris and in the Field Offices across the world.
  • Two Category 1 Institutes in the Natural Sciences:
    - Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP), Trieste, Italy; - UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS), Montreal, Canada.
  • 78 Category 2 Centres and Institutes in the Natural Sciences: operating under the auspices of UNESCO through formal arrangements approved by the UNESCO General Conference.
  • 287 UNESCO Chairs in the Natural Sciences: including distinguished professors and research experts from universities and research institutions across the globe, recognised by UNESCO.
  • 5 UNESCO Goodwill Ambassadors in the Natural Sciences: including renowned public personalities and prominent scientific profiles promoting UNESCO Natural Sciences' priorities.
